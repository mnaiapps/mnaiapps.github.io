Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

MNAi Expense Tracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your financial data is stored securely and is never accessed, collected, or sold by the developers.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced securely using Apple iCloud.

  • Data is encrypted by Apple
  • Stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Not accessible by the developer

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or track any personal or financial data.

No Third-Party Tracking

This app does not use:

  • Analytics SDKs
  • Advertising frameworks
  • Tracking tools

iCloud Sync

If you enable sync, your data is securely transmitted using Apple’s CloudKit infrastructure.

Sharing

If you use shared workspaces, your data is shared only with users you explicitly invite via iCloud.

Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete data anytime
  • Disable iCloud sync anytime
  • Revoke shared access anytime

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For any questions, contact us via the App Store support link.
