1. Introduction

Expense mnAI is a private, intelligent finance manager built exclusively for Apple devices. It is designed to give you a complete picture of your financial life — your income, spending, recurring commitments, and budget health — without ever sharing your data with advertisers, third-party analytics services, or any external server outside of Apple's own iCloud infrastructure.

What Expense mnAI does

It understands the difference between a salary deposit, a supermarket trip, and a credit card payment, and organises each one so you can understand where your money actually goes each month.

Everything you see is automated: bills are generated on their due dates, and budgets reset automatically at the start of the month.

3. Accounts

3.1 Understanding Account Types

Account types (Cash, Checking, Savings, etc.) help organize your balance sheet. You can create custom types like "Crypto Wallet" in the Setup menu.

3.2 Creating and Managing Accounts

Navigate to Accounts and tap +. Every account needs a name and a starting balance. You can mark one account as Primary to pre-select it during transaction entry.

4. Categories & Groups

Use Groups (e.g., Housing) as parent containers and Categories (e.g., Rent) as specific items. Every transaction gets both, allowing for high-level summaries and deep-dive analysis.

5. Transactions

5.1 Recording an Expense

[SCREENSHOT: Transaction Entry Form]

Assign your spend to an account and category. You can add Tags like "Business Trip" or "Holiday 2025" for cross-category tracking.

5.3 Transfers Between Accounts

Move money from Checking to Savings without affecting your Net Cash Flow. Use the "Pay From" and "Pay To" fields to adjust both balances at once.

6. Recurring Bills

Define a bill once (like Netflix or Rent), and Expense mnAI generates a "Pending" instance 30 days ahead of the due date. Mark them as paid when the money actually leaves your account.

7. Recurring Budgets

Set a target spending limit. If you enable Carry-Forward, any unspent money rolls over into the next month's allowance.

8. Reports & Analytics

8.1 Monthly Report

See Total Income vs Total Expenses. Net Cash Flow is the ultimate metric for your financial health this month.

8.3 Intelligence & Insights

AI-generated anomalies (e.g., "Grocery spend is 20% higher than average") are calculated purely on-device using local intelligence.

9. Sync & iCloud

Single Sync tracks your data across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Family Sharing enables shared workspaces for collaboration.

Subscription required for cloud features. Core tracking remains free on a single device.

10. Workspaces & Collaboration

Invite others to a shared workspace using the native iCloud Share Sheet. Collaborators can see and add transactions in real-time.

11. Privacy & Security

Zero developer access. No company servers. No third-party SDKs. Your finances are as private as your Apple ID.

12. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use it offline?

Yes. Data saves locally and syncs to iCloud whenever you are back online.

What if I cancel?

The app reverts to local-only mode. You never lose access to your historical data.
