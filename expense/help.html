Help Center

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expense mnAI. Our goal is to provide a privacy-first, powerful financial tracking experience using your own iCloud storage.

2. Accounts

Manage your Bank Accounts, Cash, and Credit Cards here. You can set opening balances and track transfers between them.

3. Categories & Groups

Organize your spending into Groups (e.g., "Housing") and Categories (e.g., "Rent"). Use icons and colors to customize your view.

4. Transactions

Record your Expenses, Income, and Transfers. You can attach receipts and add tags for deep filtering.

5. Recurring Bills

Never miss a payment. Set up recurring bills and receive notifications before they are due.

6. Recurring Budgets

Set monthly or yearly limits for specific categories. Expense mnAI tracks your "Daily Burn Rate" to help you stay on track.

7. Reports & Analytics

View deep insights into your spending patterns. Our Intelligence engine detects anomalies and highlights trends automatically.

8. Sync & Cloud

Your data is stored in your private iCloud. Enable Family Sync to collaborate with others in real-time.

9. Workspaces

Keep your Personal, Work, and Family finances completely separate using dedicated Workspaces.
